 炼金术物品, 非魔法！

      the art of alchemy has produced many useful substances that have seemingly

      miraculous properties. in truth,these materials have no magical properties

      at all. alchemists claim that their creations function strictly according

      to natural laws,and that maght be so.

 

      炼金之艺已经创造了许多似乎不可能达成之物，然则其质料中并不含丝毫魔法之力。炼金术士们主张其作品依自然之理而运作，而事实亦如他们所说。

 

      alchemical effects tend to be less potent than comparable magical items

      are typically cheap to produce. here are some recent alchemical inventions

      that adventurers of all kinds might find useful.

 

      炼金物品不如功能类似的魔法物品有效，但造价远远便宜。这里列出了一些新的炼金发明，任何冒险者都会在其中找到可用之物。

 

      Auran Mask:this mast fits over the character's nose and mouth.the bulk of

      the item is a cloth packet holding a porous,spongelike substance. it

      requires q move action to put on or remove,and once a single breath is

      taken though the mask ,its venefits last fot only 1 hour (or less if taken

      under water).

      a creature wearing an auran mask receives a +5 circumstance bonus on

      fortitude saves against inhaled toxins.in addition,the wearer can breathe

      underwater as if under the effect of a water breathing spell,though

      immersing the auran mastk in water reduces its remaining duration to 10

      minutes (or less,if less than 10 minutes remain)

 

      奥兰（？）面具：该面具覆于角色的口鼻之上，其中心是一内含多孔海绵装物质的布制包裹。此物需以一个移动等效动作戴上或移除，一旦戴上它并进行一次完整地呼吸，面具即开始生效并只能持续一个小时，若在水下使用则更短。

      戴此面具的角色在对抗吸入毒气的强韧豁免上有＋5的环境加值。另外，佩带者可以在水下呼吸，如同处于“水下呼吸”法术作用之下。浸入水中时，假面效果的持续时间缩减为10分钟（或更短，如果原本剩余时间不足）

 

      Crackle Powder:this alchemical powder creates a loud crackling noise,like

      a broomstick breaking,whenever it is jostled or struck.a single packet

      covers 5-foot-radius area;applying the powder requires a full-round

      action.once in place,the powder remains active for 1 hour and inposes a

      -10 penalty on move silently checks made when traversing the area.

 

      噼啪粉：这种炼金术粉末被挤压敲打时能产生破竹般的爆裂声。一包这样的粉末可以覆盖五英尺半径的地面，这需要一个整轮动作。当你如此做时，粉末会保留一个小时，此时从该处地面通过的潜行检定要受到－10罚值。

 

      fast torch:this item consists of a tube of waxed paper set into a short

      wooden handle.the end opposite the handle has a wax seal,and the whole

      item is about the size of a normal torch.an alchemical substance packed

      inside the tube ignites when expose to air.lighting the torch is as simple

      as removing the wax seal,which requires a swift action.

      the lit torch produces a brilliant white light that produces brihgt

      illumination in a 30-foot radius and shadowy illumination 30 feet beyond

      that.

      a fast torch burns for 10 minutes ,even in high winds or underwater(though

      it can't be ignited underwater).it's possible to extinguish a fast torch

      by burying it in sand(or a similar substance)or by grinding it out against

      a nonflammable suface.either method requires a full-round actions;once

      extinguished,it can't be relit.

 

      速燃火把（火褶子）此物由蜡纸管装在木柄上而成，蜡纸管另一端有蜡质封条，内中填满炼金物质，整体与火把一样大。揭去蜡封即可点亮火把，这是一个迅捷动作。

      点亮的火把产生明亮白光，充份照亮三十尺半径，及额外30尺昏暗照明。

      一支速燃火把可燃烧10分钟，即使在强风中或进入水下也明亮如常（但在水下无法点燃）。可以通过用沙土填埋或者将燃烧的一端在不燃硬物表面研磨来熄灭速燃火把，这需要一个整轮动作，一旦熄灭就不可再点亮。

 

      screaming flask:this container is made from thick leather with a cap sewn

      on tight and fitted with a ripcord.pulling the cord rips open the flask

      and activates the alchemical substance within.the flask emits a

      high-pitched shriek in a 15-foot cone.anything in the cone takes

      1d8　points of sonic damage and is deafend for 1 minute(fortitude DC 15

      negates).

 

      啸叫瓶：这是一个由厚皮革制成的容器，封头紧缝在口上，并且末端连接着拉绳。拉动拉绳可以打开容器并刺激里面的炼金术物品，在15尺锥装范围内发出尖利啸声，造成1d8音波伤害并耳聋一分钟（强韧豁免15，通过则无效）。

 

      weeping flask:this item looks like an empty half-gallon waterskin with an

      airtight seal.when it is opened and exposed to air,an alchemical substance

      within the flask slowly produces drinkable water.the flask produces water

      at a rate of 8 ounces per hour(becoming completely full after 8 hours).the

      water can be poured out as it appears or simply kept in the container.

 

      垂泪瓶：此物看似一个密封的半加仑空水袋。当它被打开暴露于空气时，内部的炼金术物质会缓缓产生可饮用的水，速度为8盎斯每小时，八小时后水袋会被装满。这些水可以倒出来使用或者就装在水袋里。
